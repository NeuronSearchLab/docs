## Welcome to NeuronSearchLab

NeuronSearchLab is a modern, developer-first recommendation engine that helps you deliver intelligent, personalized content to your users—at scale.

It’s built on a lightweight, serverless architecture that’s easy to integrate, simple to scale, and highly customizable. Whether you're recommending content, products, videos, or anything else, NeuronSearchLab plugs seamlessly into your stack and adapts to your use case.

### Key capabilities

- ⚡ **Real-time Recommendations** — Fast similarity searches using optimized vector queries.
- 🧠 **Contextual Intelligence** — Dynamically filter and boost results based on your business logic.
- 🔐 **Secure Access** — Built-in authentication and multi-client access control.
- 🔄 **Embeddings on Demand** — Generate and upsert item or user embeddings for high-quality personalization.
- 📈 **Event-Driven Learning** — Track and utilize user interaction data to improve recommendations over time.
