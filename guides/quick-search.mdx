Quick Search is the fastest way to move around the console without relying on the sidebar.

![Studio quick search](/images/studio-quick-search.png)

## Open quick search

- On macOS: press `Cmd + K`
- On Windows or Linux: press `Ctrl + K`
- Or click **Quick search** in the sidebar

## What it searches

Quick Search is scoped to what the signed-in user can access for their team. It searches:

- pages
- studios
- rules
- pipelines
- experiments
- segments
- users
- items

The results are grouped so you can tell whether you are jumping to a page, a studio, or a specific object.

## Best uses

Quick Search is especially useful when you need to:

- jump straight to a studio from anywhere in the console
- find a rule by name without opening the Rules page first
- open the right pipeline from a partial name
- check whether an item or user exists before running Preview or Verify

## How it fits into Studio

A common workflow is:

1. Press `Cmd/Ctrl + K`
2. Search for the surface or rule you need
3. Jump into Studio
4. Make the change
5. Preview and verify the outcome

This is faster than navigating page by page through the sidebar.
